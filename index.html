Test 04
Overnight oats recipe
Pass
Prompt Give me a recipe for making overnight oats with strawberry chunks and blueberry for topping and how to containerize it.
Output

🍓 Strawberry & Blueberry Overnight Oats — Makes 1 serving

Base Ingredients

  • Rolled oats: ½ cup (old-fashioned; avoid instant)
  • Milk of choice: ½ cup (almond, oat, soy, or dairy)
  • Greek yogurt: ¼ cup
  • Chia seeds: 1 tablespoon
  • Maple syrup or honey: 1–2 teaspoons
  • Vanilla extract: ¼ teaspoon
  • Pinch of salt

Fruit

  • Strawberries: 2–3 medium, diced into small chunks
  • Blueberries: a handful of fresh blueberries (for topping)

🥣 Instructions

  1. Mix the base: combine oats, chia seeds, and salt in your container.
  2. Add liquids: stir in milk, Greek yogurt, sweetener, and vanilla until no chia clumps remain.
  3. Fold in strawberries: gently stir in strawberry chunks.
  4. Chill: seal and refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  5. Finishing touch: top with fresh blueberries right before eating.

📦 How to Containerize

  • Best container: wide-mouth 16 oz mason jar (airtight, leak-proof, eat directly from it). Alternative: BPA-free snap-lock container.
  • Layering for meal prep: bottom = oat mixture · middle = strawberry chunks · top = blueberries in a separate small bag.
  • Storage life: up to 5 days in the fridge.
  • Portability tip: if oats are too thick, stir in a splash of milk. Pack a reusable spoon and granola on the side for crunch.
